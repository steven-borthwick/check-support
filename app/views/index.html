Support for Check your State Pension

Prototypes

Voluntary National Insurance contributions

Problem: agents can't easily advice users if they can improve their pension by paying gaps. It involves a separate tool and a call back, even when some users can't improve their pension.

Hypothesis: showing agents if users can improve their pension with clear info and simple messaging will mean that they can answer enquiries at the point of contact.

Date: 26 June 2017

State Pension age calculator

Problem: agents can't easily get the State Pension age to answer enquiries from users and have to use a separate tool.

Hypothesis: allowing agents to enter a Date of Birth inside the service will make it easier and quicker for agents to answer these enquiries.

Date: 26 June 2017

Third parties

Problem: agents need to send out statements to third parties, they can't do this easily or consistently.

Hypothesis: allowing agents to choose who the forecasts are sent to and showing fields specific to third party details will make it faster and easier to use. We'll also get less errors and more consistent results.
